What is golf?

  • Golf is a club-and-ball sport in which players use various clubs to hit balls into a series of holes on a course in as few strokes as possible
  • A typical course contains 18 holes
    • A hole starts from the tee box and ends on the putting green where the actual hole or cup is located
    • A hole can contain various hazards like water, rocks, sand traps (better known as bunkers)
    • Each hole on a course is unique in its specific layout and arragement
